Video Foam

Dont'cha just hate when your kids, dog, and grandma won't listen to you? Well, now you can make at least your videos responsive using Video Foam!
Note, Video Foam has graduated into the product, and you can find it in the embed options for your video. This lab is still useful if you want to apply video foam to all of the embeds on a page.
Play around with the example to see how it works, then clear it and paste in your own embed code! Just paste in a Wistia embed code and start customizing, or show me an example.

One video or all of them?

The Video Foam plugin can target a single embed code of your choosing, or all Wistia videos on a page.
